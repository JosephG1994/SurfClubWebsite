Pacific Surf Club

Pacific Surf Club is the place for surfing enthusiasts of all ages and abilities. Situated in San Francisco's Ocean Beach, experience the waves of the Pacific Ocean in our welcoming surfing community.

Every weekend we host surfing meetups, competitions, and special events that allow our members to come together and form friendships.

Pacific Surf Club also provides surfing lessons to members of all ages. We provide youth (age 6-12) and teen/adult (age 13+) lessons in the beginning and advanced levels.

About Us

We have been operating Pacific Surf Club for over 30 years. Originally a family group, we have grown in size to over 100 members of all ages and surfing abilities.

We strive to provide the best surfing experience in a friendly community setting, welcoming members into our big family of surfers, whether you're a family or an individual surfer.

Becoming a member of Pacific Surf Club will be the best decision you make. We provide affordable pricing for families and individuals and provide plenty of perks that will make your membership valuable.
